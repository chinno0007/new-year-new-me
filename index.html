My failures of previous year

People fail at some point. We all do. But this is an important part of life. While the definition of failure is the “lack of success to something such as an enterprise, or deficient in a desirable quality,” it is not always correct. One of the old sayings, “failure is the path to success,” still holds true even in the current world. Failure should not be considered a means to an end or tagged with permanence. It means missing a target, like in a darts game, but it does not prevent you from trying again. Therefore, when you are faced with the task of writing an essay on failure, how do you go about it?
